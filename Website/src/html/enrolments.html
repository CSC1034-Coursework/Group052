Report 1: Score Improvement Analysis

Compares average pre- and post-assessment scores across programmes, courses, and gender groups. Use this report to identify which programmes produce the greatest measurable learning improvement and whether outcomes differ by gender.

Report 2: Funding Risk & Coverage

Each programme has a planned budget — the total cost of delivery. Confirmed Funding shows how much has actually been committed by donors. The Shortfall is the gap between the two. A programme is flagged AT RISK when its funding has expired or when no funding is confirmed, meaning delivery may be disrupted. Cost per Participant shows financial efficiency — a lower figure means more people are reached per pound spent.

Report 3: Attendance & Completion by Region

Breaks down enrolment completion and attendance rates by programme and region. Use this to identify where participants are disengaging and which regions may need additional support or scheduling adjustments.
