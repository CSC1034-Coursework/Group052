What it does

One system for the full programme lifecycle

Many organisations running gender equality programmes manage data across spreadsheets, paper forms, and disconnected tools. EmpowerTrack brings it all together — so coordinators spend less time on admin and more time on impact.

  • Register and track beneficiaries across regions and demographics
  • Enrol participants in courses and record session attendance
  • Monitor pre and post-assessment scores to measure learning
  • Link funding sources to programmes and track budget coverage
  • Generate reports that answer real organisational questions

SDG alignment

5
Gender Equality

EmpowerTrack supports UN SDG 5 by enabling organisations to monitor access to gender equality programmes, track outcomes across demographics, and produce the evidence base needed for sustained funding and policy change.

Key features

Beneficiary management

Record who is being served — age group, region, education level, employment status — and track their full programme journey.

Programme tracking

Manage programmes across regions, assign staff teams, link funding, and schedule sessions with attendance logging.

SQL reports & charts

Parameterised reports using multi-table JOINs and aggregates. Live Chart.js visualisations from real database data.

Staff coordination

Track coordinators, trainers, and volunteers by role, specialisation, region, and certification status.

Funding monitoring

Link government, NGO, UN, and private funding to specific programmes. Track active status and budget allocation.

Assessment tracking

Compare pre and post-assessment scores to measure learning gain per course, programme, and beneficiary group.

Who uses it

RC

Regional coordinator

Creates programme records, manages enrolments, logs session attendance, and monitors completion across their area.

DA

Data analyst

Runs parameterised reports to identify underserved demographics, track assessment improvement, and compare programmes.

FO

Funding officer

Reviews funding allocation reports, monitors budgets across programmes, and verifies impact for donor reporting.
